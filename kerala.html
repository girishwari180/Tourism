About Kerala

Kerala is a beautiful state on India’s southwestern coast, often called God’s Own Country. It is famous for its serene backwaters, lush green hills, and long palm-lined beaches. Kerala has a rich culture shaped by classical dance forms, Ayurveda, and vibrant festivals. The state is also known for its high literacy rate and harmonious blend of nature and tradition.
